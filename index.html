Hack-A-Thon 8.0 - Project (Bangalore - 31/04/2023)- Food Blogger Website

Introduction:

"Hello everyone! We are Team FoodiesConnect, and we're thrilled to present our food blogging website, 'FlavorVoyage,' which promises to take food enthusiasts on a delectable journey of taste and discovery."

Target Audience:

"FlavorVoyage is designed for food lovers of all ages, from amateur home cooks to seasoned gourmets, who want to explore diverse cuisines, try exciting recipes, and share their culinary experiences with a like-minded community."

    Key Features:

  • 00 Technology Stack:

    For the development of FlavorVoyage, we utilized HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the front-end, while Python with Node Js framework powers the back-end. The website's data is stored in a Django database, ensuring seamless and efficient data management

  • 01 Interactive Recipe Repository:

    Our website boasts an extensive collection of handpicked recipes from around the world. From mouth-watering desserts to exotic main courses, users can easily browse and access detailed recipes with clear instructions.

  • 02 User-Generated Content:

    FlavorVoyage fosters a vibrant community where users can contribute their own culinary creations. Whether it's a family heirloom recipe or a unique fusion dish, everyone can share their passion for food and inspire others to try something new.

  • 03 Personalized Recommendations:

    With our AI-driven recommendation system, users will receive personalized recipe suggestions based on their dietary preferences, cooking skills, and previous interactions on the website. This ensures a delightful and tailored experience for each user.

 

Tech-A-Thon Project (Jalandhar punjab - 03/09/2022)- Blog Portal Website

Introduction:

Blogify is a blog portal website that aims to provide a platform for bloggers to share their content and readers to discover and engage with various blog posts. The project will be built using modern web technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript for the front-end, and Node.js with Express.js for the back-end. MongoDB will be used as the database to store blogs, user information, and comments.

    Key Features:

  • 00 Blog Listing and Search:

    The homepage of Blogify will display a list of featured or recent blog posts from various bloggers. Readers can explore blogs by categories or use the search functionality to find specific topics or authors.

  • 01 User Authentication:

    Participants will implement a user registration and login system to allow bloggers to create accounts and access their dashboards. Readers can also create accounts to follow bloggers and leave comments.

  • 02 Dashboard for Bloggers:

    After logging in, bloggers will have access to their dashboard where they can create, edit, and delete their blog posts. They can view their published blogs and see the number of views and comments on each post.

  • 03 Blog Publishing:

    Bloggers can write and publish new blog posts from their dashboard. Each blog post will have a title, content, and optional tags for categorization.
